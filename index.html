The story of Pat's Salmon Cookies

Cookies made with fish? Yes, it's a thing! Shaped like Salmon but still a cookie, Pat's Salmon cookies are the perfect coffee-time confection. They are both savory and sweet, with a hint of fishy goodness. Pat's Salmon Cookies started as one small stand on Alki Beach and has since opened locations all over the map.
Read on to learn how Pat landed a spot on the map of Seattle's top places to visit and why Seattleites give Pat's stands two thumbs up!
